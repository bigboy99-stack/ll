Domestic Scaffolding Hire

Reliable, safe, and respectful access solutions for your home renovation and repair projects.

Whether you are painting your fascias, repairing a chimney, or building an extension, we provide the safest platform to get the job done. We pride ourselves on being punctual, tidy, and careful to protect your garden and driveway during every installation.

  • Extension Scaffolding

    Designed for builders and homeowners alike, providing multiple levels of safe access for brickwork and roofing on new extensions.

  • Roof & Chimney Access

    Specialized platforms that allow roofers and repointers to work safely on steep pitches without damaging your roof tiles.

  • Painting & Decorating

    Lightweight, stable towers and walkways that make exterior decorating faster and much safer than using traditional ladders.

  • Fascias & Soffits

    Continuous wrap-around scaffolding that gives tradespeople easy access to the entire roofline for guttering and fascia replacements.

The Homeowner Promise

We treat your home like our own. Our domestic service includes:

  • Property Protection: We use base plates and padding to protect your lawn, driveway, and walls.
  • Fixed Pricing: No hidden fees. The price we quote for hire and "strike" (removal) is what you pay.
  • Fully Insured: Comprehensive Public Liability insurance for your peace of mind.
  • Punctual Service: We arrive on time and dismantle the scaffold as soon as your project is finished.
